MINIMUM ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

In order to be admitted to a program leading to a Diploma of College Studies (DEC), applicants must possess a Quebec Secondary School Diploma or have completed a level of education that is deemed equivalent by the College. Applicants must also have successfully completed Mathematics TS 5 or SN 5. Students who have Math CST 4 will also be considered and, if admitted, will take the equivalent to Math TS 5 or SN 5 as an extra course. For detailed admissions requirements please refer to the Admissions Handbook.

ADMISSION PRIORITIES

Even if candidates to programs meet the requirements of the Règlement sur le regime des études collégiales, they may be refused admission because of the lack of space.

APPLICATION DEADLINE

WHO SHOULD APPLY

The Computer Science program is designed for students who: are fascinated by computer-related topics enjoy working with others are generally curious and enjoy looking up information enjoy problem-solving approach situations in an organized and logical manner are able to solve word problems in mathematics love to learn and wish to keep learning appreciate the role that computers play in our lives and that machines serve us, not vice versa!
